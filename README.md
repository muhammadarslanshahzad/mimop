## Project Description

Cleaning is boring. Robots are usually boring too. MiMoMop changes that. It mixes **robotics + AI + humor + gamification** to make cleaning fun and interactive. It remembers your habits, adapts its humor, and gently nudges you to keep your space tidy while doing all the work itself. Think of it as a tiny, opinionated roommate that never complains but always keeps your space clean.
---

## Features

* **Autonomous Cleaning:** Floors, surfaces, edges, and corners.
* **Manipulation:** Pick up small objects, push obstacles, wipe tables.
* **Navigation:** SLAM-based, multi-room, avoids obstacles.
* **LLM-Powered Interaction:** Understands your commands, cracks jokes, plans tasks.
* **Gamification:** XP, streaks, achievements, mini-challenges.
* **Personalities:** Sarcastic, Cute & Clumsy, Drill Sergeant, Overly Proud, Chill Minimalist.
* **Memory:** Remembers rooms, habits, humor preferences.
* **Offline & Safe:** No cloud, respects privacy, emergency stop, safe force limits.
